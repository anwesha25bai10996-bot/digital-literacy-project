# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

✅ DO'S
1. Keep your profiles professional and up to date
2. Think before you post — once online, always online
3. Use privacy settings to control who sees your content
4. Engage respectfully in online discussions
5. Share academic achievements and projects professionally

❌ DON'TS
1. Don't share personal information like address or phone number
2. Don't post offensive, racist or inappropriate content
3. Don't forward unverified news or fake information
4. Don't accept friend requests from unknown people
5. Don't share your passwords or OTPs with anyone
