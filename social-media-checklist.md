Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

✅ DO'S	
1. Keep profiles professional and updated	"Maintain accurate information and a clean, consistent image across all platforms."
2. Think before posting	Remember that online content is permanent and can affect future opportunities.
3. Use privacy settings wisely	"Control who can view posts, photos, and personal details."
4. Engage respectfully	Participate in discussions with courtesy and professionalism.
5. Share academic achievements	"Highlight projects, awards, and experiences that reflect positively on academic and career goals."
   
❌ DON'TS	
1. Avoid sharing personal information	"Never post addresses, phone numbers, or sensitive details publicly."
2. Don’t post offensive or inappropriate content	"Refrain from sharing racist, sexist, or disrespectful material."
3. Don’t spread unverified information	Verify sources before sharing news or updates.
4. Don’t accept unknown friend requests	Connect only with people who are known and trusted.
5. Don’t share passwords or OTPs	Keep login credentials private to protect accounts from misuse.
