# Stay Safe Online — Prevention Checklist
## For College Students in India

###  General Online Safety
1. Use unique and strong passwords for all accounts and do not use the same password
2. Use two-factor authentication (2FA) for all critical accounts
3. Don’t click on suspicious URLs if they reach you via SMS, WhatsApp, or email
4. Verify the authenticity of people who request personal details
5. Keep your phone and apps updated to the latest version
6. Don’t share personal details like Aadhaar number and PAN with unverified websites

###  UPI & Financial Safety
7. Don’t share UPI PIN and OTP with anyone 
   — not even bank officials
8. Remember: Receiving money does not require entering PIN. If someone asks for PIN to send money, then you are actually sending money and not receiving money

###  Social Media Safety
9. Keep all social media profiles private
10. Don’t accept friend requests from unknown people

###  If You Are a Victim
- Immediately report to: cybercrime.gov.in
- Call Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (24x7)
- Block the fraudster and take screenshots as evidence
- Inform your bank immediately to freeze the transaction
